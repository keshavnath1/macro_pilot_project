## Revised Milestone and Progress Update

The **Data Science team has developed a good working knowledge of DVC** and how it can be applied to both current and future model workflows. The initial focus was placed on DVC adoption, particularly for workflows such as **Refit** and **Emulator**, so that the team could establish a practical understanding of data and artifact versioning before expanding into broader pipeline orchestration capabilities.

Through this work, the team has gained experience with **single-stage execution, multi-stage pipelines, input and output dependencies, artifact management, and DVC pipeline visualization**. This knowledge can support the current workflows while also providing a useful foundation for future projects.

## Kubeflow Progress

Unlike DVC, **Kubeflow has not yet been fully adopted**. The team is currently making progress toward understanding how Kubeflow Pipelines could be used effectively and how it may complement the DVC-based workflow.

Kubeflow was introduced after the initial DVC work, with a particular focus on **execution lineage**. This includes gaining visibility into pipeline runs, execution status, logs, metrics, and the outputs produced by individual stages.

Given the current timeline, it may not be possible to explore every Kubeflow capability or complete adoption to its fullest extent. However, the team expects to build a practical understanding of how Kubeflow can support pipeline execution and execution lineage. This initial learning could provide a foundation for broader adoption in future work.

## Overall Lineage Approach

The evolving approach can be summarized as follows:

| Lineage area | Platform | Current position |
|---|---|---|
| **Code lineage** | Git | Used to track changes to code and configuration |
| **Data and artifact lineage** | DVC | Adopted, with useful knowledge established for current and future workflows |
| **Execution lineage** | Kubeflow | Currently being explored, with adoption efforts focused on pipeline execution, logs, metrics, and run history |

Overall, the team’s primary milestone has been the successful development of practical DVC knowledge. The current Kubeflow effort is a gradual next step toward connecting **code lineage, data lineage, and execution lineage** within a more complete workflow.
